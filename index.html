Each day, we'll hide a new Mystery City in Europe. Your goal is to guess the city from the clue using the fewest number of guesses. Each incorrect guess will appear on the grid with a colour, indicating how close you are to the Mystery City. The hotter the colour, the closer your guess is to the answer!

A new Mystery City will be available every day! Are you ready to explore Europe and test your geography knowledge?
